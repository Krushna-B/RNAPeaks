# RNAPeaks ![](reference/figures/logo.png)

![R](https://img.shields.io/badge/R-%3E%3D4.1-276DC3?style=flat-square&logo=r&logoColor=white)![Bioconductor](https://img.shields.io/badge/Bioconductor-3.17+-87B13F?style=flat-square)![License:

RNAPeaks is an R package for publication-quality visualization of
RNA-binding protein peaks overlaid on annotated gene structures, with
statistical analysis of binding frequency around splice junctions.

**Full documentation, function reference, and articles at
[krushna-b.github.io/RNAPeaks](https://krushna-b.github.io/RNAPeaks/).**
